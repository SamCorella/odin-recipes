Tomato Soup

Description

A creamy tomato soup recipe that only requires a handful of ingredients and is incredibly simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 quart tomato juice
  • 2 cups milk
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Sautee onions and butter until onions are translucent.
  2. Stir in flour until no lumps remain, then slowly whisk in the tomato juice.
  3. Add salt to taste.
  4. Cook until boiling, then remove from heat.
  5. Slowly stir in milk.
  6. Serve immediately.
